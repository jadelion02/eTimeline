Finding the Day of the Week Examples:

1.      2022-09-15

The number intersecting 2022 and S (September) is 3

Add 3 to 15 = 18

18 Mod 7 = 4 (4th day of the week)

Therefore 2022-09-15 is Thursday.

 

2.      2023-08-02

The number intersecting 2023 and A (August) is 1

Add 1 to 2 = 3

3 Mod 7 = 3 (3rd day of the week)

Therefore 2023-08-02 is Wednesday.

 

3.      2044-03-06

The number intersecting 2044 and M (March) is 1

Add 1 to 6 = 7

7 Mod 7 = 0 (0 day of the week)

Therefore 2044-03-06 is Sunday.
